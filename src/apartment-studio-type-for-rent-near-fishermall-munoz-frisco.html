Located in the heart of the Philippine archipelago in the Luzon region is a metropolis blooming with color and vibrancy. Metro Manila is also home to various transportation means. The Ninoy Aquino International Airport is located in both Paranaque City and Pasay City. Extensive roadways, such as the Epifanio Delos Santos Avenue (EDSA) North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and South Luzon Expressway (SLEX), which connect cities and neighboring provinces are found in Metro Manila.
